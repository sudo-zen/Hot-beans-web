What we offer

We’re an integrated creative agency with a truly diverse roster of clients. A tight team with specialisms across the spectrum. Our work can be found everywhere from broadband providers and supermarket shelves to tourist boards and boutique gyms. We’re looking for a Junior Web Dev with at least 1-2 years of experience and a portfolio to prove it, ready to join a digital team taking on new projects and supporting existing clients. You’ll work alongside a Senior Developer and Digital Manager on everything from entirely digital projects to truly integrated briefs stretching across creative, PR and social media. For now, we’re working from home – you’ll be interviewed remotely, and should expect an initial period of work to also be home-based. But once we have safe Covid-19 protocols in place, we’re looking to return to our office in Leeds Dock. When things are back to normal, you’ll enjoy all sorts of benefits, including inspiration days, duvet days, holiday buyback, a summer festival, 5-a-side football and PT sessions. You must have… A working understanding of HTML, CSS and JS/jQuery Knowledge of Bootstrap / Foundation, or other CSS Frameworks Basic PHP knowledge WordPress / Other CMS development experience The ability to debug front-end issues using the inspector tool Great attention to detail and problem-solving skills You might have… WordPress plugin development and maintenance experience Experience working on eCommerce sites Knowledge of Javascript Frameworks (React, Vue) Experience of GIT or other version control Basic SEO skills.
